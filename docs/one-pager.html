The Problem

Digital classrooms exploded, but prep is still manual. Educators burn hours turning PDFs into quizzes & visuals, while learners juggle disjointed tools and rarely collaborate outside live sessions. Sharing assessments is clunky—no simple way to publish a quiz link, collect attempts, and spot trends without spreadsheets.

Our Solution

  • Auto-built study kits: Feed Progrezz any PDF or link and instantly spin up quizzes, flashcards, essay drills, and visual mind maps. Regenerations focus on the exact gaps.
  • Visual knowledge maps: Color-coded, left-to-right canvases with expandable layers for workshops and async storytelling.
  • Sharable quizzes with live results: Publish with a share code so anyone can take it—no login. Creators watch results roll in and export leaderboards.
  • Unified Student Hub: Projects keep uploads, generated content, and PDF chats in one feed, complete with job alerts and suggested next steps.
  • Feedback-aware loop: Automated reminders resurface weak topics; mind maps include actionable next steps for guided revision.
