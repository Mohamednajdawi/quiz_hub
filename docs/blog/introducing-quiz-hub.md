## What is Progrezz?

Progrezz is an AI-powered educational platform that automatically generates personalized study content from your materials. Think of it as your personal study assistant that never sleeps, creating quizzes, flashcards, and essay questions tailored to your learning needs.

### The Core Concept

Upload a PDF or paste a URL. Our AI analyzes the content, understands the key concepts, and generates:

- **Interactive Quizzes** with multiple-choice questions at your preferred difficulty level
- **Flashcard Sets** optimized for memorization and spaced repetition
- **Essay Q&A** with detailed answers and key information points
- **AI Chat** that lets you ask questions directly to your uploaded documents

All of this happens in seconds, powered by advanced language models that understand context, nuance, and educational best practices.

## Technical Deep Dive (For the Curious)

### How Content Generation Works

1. **Content Ingestion**: PDFs are parsed and URLs are scraped to extract text content
2. **AI Analysis**: Our language model analyzes the content for:
   - Key concepts and themes
   - Important facts and details
   - Relationships between ideas
   - Appropriate question types
3. **Generation**: Questions are generated using educational templates optimized for:
   - Clarity and accuracy
   - Appropriate difficulty
   - Educational value
   - Distractor quality (for multiple-choice)
4. **Validation**: Generated content is validated for quality and relevance
5. **Delivery**: Content is formatted and delivered to the user interface

### Architecture Highlights

- **Microservices-Ready**: Backend and frontend are fully decoupled
- **API-First Design**: RESTful APIs enable future integrations
- **Database Migrations**: Schema evolution without data loss
- **Webhook Integration**: Real-time payment and subscription updates
- **Rate Limiting**: Fair usage policies to ensure quality for all users

### Security & Privacy

- **JWT Authentication**: Secure, stateless authentication
- **Input Validation**: All user inputs are validated and sanitized
- **File Storage**: Secure, isolated storage for user uploads
- **Data Encryption**: Sensitive data encrypted at rest and in transit
- **GDPR Compliance**: Full compliance with European data protection regulations
